Neuronic circuits

Inspired by nandgame

Click to create a neuron. Shift click to create an inhibitory neuron. Right click to activate and send a action potential through the neuron. Escape to unselect a neuron, and backspace on a selected neuron to delete it.

1. The goal for this level is to make the neuron on the left fire every other time an action potential is sent to the neuron on top.

2. Create a circuit so that an action potential will toggle whether or not a loop is sent to the output neuron

3. Create a circuit so that an action potential will be sent if an action potential is sent from the other input
