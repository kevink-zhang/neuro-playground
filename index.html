Neuronic circuits

Inspired by nandgame

Click to create a neuron. Shift click to create an inhibitory neuron. Right click to activate and send a action potential through the neuron. Escape to unselect a neuron, and backspace on a selected neuron to delete it.

The goal for this level is to prevent the neuron at the top from firing. You are not allowed to move any of the initial neurons. Create a new neuron and right click it to send an action potential. Creating an action potential starts the level (or at least it should once we get around to it)
